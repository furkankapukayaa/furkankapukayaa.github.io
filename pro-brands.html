Prodent, Prosoll & Prosense - Corporate Website

I developed a unified and professional corporate website tailored for a brand family consisting of Prodent, Prosoll, and Prosense. This platform acts as their central digital identity.

The main goal of this project was to establish a cohesive online presence where they can effectively showcase their diverse product lines, provide a clear view of their sales points, and highlight their corporate structure and communication channels. This solution provides potential customers with a seamless and informative digital experience.


Technologies Used

  • CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • Bootstrap
  • Responsive Design
