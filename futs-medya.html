Futs Medya - Corporate Website & Internal Panel

Delivered a dual-purpose digital solution for Futs Medya. We created an engaging promotional website to showcase their media and design portfolio, paired with a customized internal management panel.

This panel was specifically designed to oversee internal business processes, track financial performance, and manage personnel efficiently. The project also involved bespoke graphic design work to perfectly align the platform with their modern brand identity.
